## The Memory Moat

Most AI assistants have the memory of a goldfish. Alice has institutional knowledge.

**4-Layer Memory Architecture:**

```
Layer 1: Daily Notes (memory/YYYY-MM-DD.md)
         Raw events, decisions, extracted facts. Ground truth.

Layer 2: Long-Term Memory (MEMORY.md)
         Curated lessons, relationship context, operating rules.
         "Every failure becomes a permanent rule."

Layer 3: Solutions Library (memory/solutions/)
         Reusable patterns from past problem-solving.
         Before starting any task: "Have we solved something like this before?"

Layer 4: Compound Learning (Weekly Synthesis)
         Cross-session pattern recognition. Learnings pushed back
         into agent configs. The system literally gets smarter every week.
```

**Plus: World State Cache** — a materialized view of Notion + email + Slack + calendar, unified into a single queryable source. When Alice drafts an email, she knows your sprint status, your calendar conflicts, your vendor relationships, and what was discussed in Slack yesterday. No other AI assistant on the market has this.
